Core Competencies
My expertise spans the full pipeline of environment creation, from initial concept to final implementation. My technical proficiency is strongest in Unreal Engine 5, where I specialize in terrain sculpting, lighting, and Blueprint scripting to bring worlds to life. My core strengths lie in environment and level design, visual storytelling, seamless gameplay integration, performance optimization, and collaborative teamwork.

Education

This creative training is underpinned by two years of foundational studies in Internet Engineering at the University of Rome Tor Vergata, which instilled in me a methodical and analytical approach to problem-solving.

Project Highlight: Dimitria (Cooperative Fantasy RPG)
As the Environment Artist and Level Designer for this project, I was entrusted with the end-to-end creation of a semi-open world. My mission was to build a cohesive and diverse landscape that supported the game's narrative and cooperative mechanics. I directed the art and established the mood for all key areas, from the awe-inspiring Cliffside Landing to the tense and eerie forest and the climactic Final Boss Arena. A significant part of my role involved ensuring technical excellence; I optimized performance through meticulous LOD management and Blueprint debugging. Furthermore, I actively supported combat design by participating in playtesting, tuning difficulty, and designing environmental setups for engaging encounters. The achievement was the successful delivery of a fully realized game world that balanced high visual quality with smooth performance.
